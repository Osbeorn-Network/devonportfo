Osbeorn

A couple of years ago, I started my business called Osbeorn. At the time, we just had a small Minecraft server running on an old computer I had sitting, collecting dust. Soon, once we started to grow, I purchased a Dell PowerEdge R410 server. This was by far one of the best decisions I had ever made. What this server would allow us to do would change my life for ever. This server allowed me to host multiple Minecraft servers for other people and earn me money so that I could grow. I soon met Jonas and Alex who would also help me get this thing started!
